Gammy and The Living Things

Nigeria's First feature length 3D animated musical, ‘Gammy and The Living Things’ follows the story of a young red-head lizard and his music band. This young red-head agama lizard's dreams of becoming a star are put through several tests as he embarks on a journey together with his band that starts off in the Hinterland Basin teeming with a wide variety of animal life and crisscrosses into the bustling, but dangerous city of Lagos, Nigeria.
